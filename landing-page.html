Background

An Economics graduate from Kenyatta University and innate desire to grow and push self past the limits. Also studied Economics and Statistics at Kwara State University, Nigeria and experienced a tonne of cultures etc. I also worked at Equity Bank Limited after high school; a rare experience that allowed me to interact with professional environment at a young age. The better part of my days are spent writing for charity and money.And now writing code for money.Maybe charity.

My whole life has been guided by the constant motivation to make things that make a difference. The software development journey, at Moringa School in particular, has finally handed me the opportunity to engage my creativity, constantly challenge myself, and most importantly LEARN!
